# How to Book Your Stay

Booking your stay is easy! Just follow these simple steps.

## 1. Select Your Dates

*   Use the calendar to select your desired **check-in** and **check-out** dates.
*   Available dates will be shown in black, while unavailable dates will be grayed out.
*   The total price for your selected date range will be calculated and displayed automatically.

## 2. Enter Your Information

*   Once you have selected your dates, a short form will appear.
*   Please enter your **full name**, **email address**, and **phone number**.
*   This information is required to secure your booking and to contact you with important updates.

## 3. Choose a Payment Method

You can choose from one of the following payment options:

*   **Credit Card**: Pay instantly and securely with your credit card. Your booking will be confirmed immediately.
*   **Bank Transfer**: Reserve your dates now and pay later via a direct bank transfer. You will receive an email with payment instructions, and your booking will be held in a "Pending" state until the payment is received.

## 4. Confirm Your Booking

*   Review your booking details one last time.
*   Click the **"Book Now"** button to complete your reservation.
*   You will receive a confirmation email shortly with all the details of your stay.

That's it! We look forward to hosting you.
