# First Login

Once your email is verified, you can log in to your BookBed account for the first time.

1.  **Go to the login page:** Open your browser and go to [bookbed.io/login](https://bookbed.io/login).
2.  **Enter your credentials:**
    *   **Email Address:** Enter the email you used to register.
    *   **Password:** Enter the password you created.
3.  **Click "Login":** You'll be taken to your dashboard.

## Login Options

*   **Forgot Password:** If you can't remember your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link to reset it.
*   **Social Logins:** You can also log in using your Google or Apple account for quicker access.
*   **Remember Me:** Check this box if you want to stay logged in on your current device.

## Your First Dashboard View

The first time you log in, you'll see your main dashboard. We recommend taking a quick tour to familiarize yourself with the main features.
