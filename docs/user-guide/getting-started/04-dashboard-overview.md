# Dashboard Overview

Welcome to your BookBed dashboard! This is your central hub for managing your properties, bookings, and settings.

## Main Navigation

The main navigation menu on the left side (on desktop) or at the bottom (on mobile) allows you to access all the key sections of the app:

*   **Dashboard:** An overview of your key metrics and recent activity.
*   **Calendar:** A timeline view of your bookings.
*   **Bookings:** A list of all your past, present, and future bookings.
*   **Properties:** Manage your properties and units.
*   **Settings:** Configure your account and notification settings.

## Dashboard Metrics

The dashboard provides a quick look at your performance:

*   **Total Bookings:** The total number of bookings you've received.
*   **Occupancy Rate:** The percentage of your available days that are booked.
*   **Total Revenue:** Your total earnings from bookings.

## Recent Activity

This section shows you a feed of your most recent bookings, inquiries, and other important events.
