# BookBed User Guide

Welcome to the BookBed user guide! This documentation is designed to help you get the most out of our platform.

## Quick Start Checklist

New to BookBed? Follow our [Quick Start Checklist](./quick-start.md) to get your property up and running in no time.

*   **[Getting Started](./getting-started/)**: Learn how to create your account, verify your email, and take a tour of the dashboard.
*   **[Property Management](./properties/)**: Find out how to add and manage your properties and units.
*   **[Pricing](./pricing/)**: Set up your pricing, including base rates and custom daily prices.
*   **[Calendar & Bookings](./calendar/)**: Manage your bookings, block dates, and sync with other calendars.
*   **[Widget Integration](./widget/)**: Integrate the BookBed booking widget into your website.
*   **[Payments](./payments/)**: Set up Stripe and other payment methods.
*   **[Settings & Profile](./settings/)**: Manage your account settings, notifications, and security.
*   **[Troubleshooting](./troubleshooting/)**: Find answers to common questions and solutions to common problems.
