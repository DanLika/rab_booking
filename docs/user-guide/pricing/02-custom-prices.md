# Custom Daily Prices

For dates that require a different price from your base rate, such as weekends, holidays, or special events, you can set custom daily prices.

## How to Set a Custom Price for a Single Day

1.  **Go to the Pricing Tab:** In the Unit Hub, navigate to the "Pricing" tab. You will see a calendar view of your prices.
2.  **Select a Date:** Click on the date for which you want to set a custom price.
3.  **Enter the New Price:** In the panel that appears, enter the new price for that day.
4.  **Save:** Click "Save" or "Apply". The calendar will update to show the new price.

## How to Set a Custom Price for a Date Range

1.  **Select a Start Date:** Click on the first day of the date range you want to change.
2.  **Select an End Date:** Click on the last day of the range.
3.  **Enter the New Price:** In the panel, enter the price that will apply to every day in the selected range.
4.  **Save:** Click "Save" or "Apply".

## Pricing Strategy Tips

*   **Weekend Pricing:** Set higher prices for Fridays and Saturdays.
*   **Seasonal Pricing:** Increase prices during your high season and for local holidays.
*   **Last-Minute Deals:** Consider lowering prices for unbooked dates that are coming up soon to attract last-minute guests.
