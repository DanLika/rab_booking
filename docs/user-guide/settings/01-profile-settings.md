# Profile Settings Guide

Keeping your profile information up-to-date is important for professional communication with guests and for managing your business details.

You can access your profile by clicking on your name or profile icon in the top right corner of the dashboard and selecting "Profile".

## Editing Your Personal Information

*   **Profile Photo:** Upload a professional-looking photo of yourself or your company logo. This helps build trust with guests.
*   **Name:** You can update your first and last name.
*   **Contact Info:** Keep your email address and phone number current so we can reach you with important account updates.

## Company Details

If you are operating as a business, you can add your company's information here.
*   **Company Name:** The legal name of your business.
*   **Address:** Your business address.
*   **Tax ID / VAT Number:** Your business registration or tax identification number.

This information may be used for invoicing or other official communication.

## Saving Changes

After making any updates to your profile, be sure to click the **"Save"** or **"Update Profile"** button at the bottom of the page. Your changes will not be applied until you do.
