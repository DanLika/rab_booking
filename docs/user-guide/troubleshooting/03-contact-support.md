# Contacting Support

We're here to help! If you've run into an issue you can't solve with our documentation or have any questions, please reach out to our support team.

## How to Contact Us

*   **Email Support:** The best way to reach us is by email at **info@bookbed.io**.

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all support requests within **24 business hours**. Please be patient, and we will get back to you as soon as we can.

## What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us resolve your issue as quickly as possible, please include the following information in your email:

*   **Your Account Email:** The email address you use to log in to BookBed.
*   **A Clear Subject Line:** For example, "Issue with iCal Sync" or "Question about payment settings".
*   **Detailed Description of the Problem:**
    *   What were you trying to do?
    *   What did you expect to happen?
    *   What actually happened? Please include the exact text of any error messages you saw.
*   **Screenshots:** A picture is worth a thousand words! If you can, please include a screenshot of the issue.
*   **Booking or Unit ID (if applicable):** If your issue relates to a specific booking or unit, please include its ID.

### Support Request Template

You can use this template for your email:

```
**Subject:** [Your Subject Here]

**My Account Email:** [Your Email Here]

**Description of Issue:**
[Explain the problem in detail here.]

**Steps I've already tried:**
[List anything you've already tried to fix it.]

**Booking ID (if relevant):** [ID Here]
**Unit ID (if relevant):** [ID Here]
```

## Bug Reporting and Feature Requests

*   **Bug Reports:** If you think you've found a bug, please provide as much detail as possible so our developers can investigate and fix it.
*   **Feature Requests:** We love hearing your ideas for how to make BookBed better! Feel free to send us your suggestions.

Thank you for helping us improve BookBed!
