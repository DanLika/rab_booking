# Widget Overview

The BookBed Widget is a powerful tool that you can embed directly into your own website. It allows guests to check availability, see prices, and book their stay without ever leaving your site. This provides a seamless booking experience for your guests and helps you convert more website visitors into direct bookings.

## Benefits of Using the Widget

*   **Increase Direct Bookings:** Avoid paying commissions to third-party booking sites.
*   **Seamless Guest Experience:** Guests can book without being redirected to another website.
*   **Always Up-to-Date:** The widget's availability is synced in real-time with your BookBed calendar.
*   **Customizable:** You can tailor the widget to match your website's style and your business needs.

## Widget Modes Explained

The widget can be configured to operate in one of three modes, giving you control over how bookings are made.

| Mode                  | Description                                                                          | Best For                                                                 |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Calendar Only**     | Displays an availability calendar. Guests can see open dates but cannot book online.   | Owners who prefer to handle all booking inquiries via phone or email.    |
| **Booking with Approval** | Guests can request to book specific dates. The booking is held until you manually approve it. | Owners who want to screen guests or confirm availability before finalizing. |
| **Instant Booking**   | Guests can select dates, pay, and instantly confirm their booking online.            | Owners who want a fully automated booking process, similar to major OTAs.  |

You can choose the mode that best fits your workflow in the [Widget Setup](./02-widget-setup.md) screen.

## How Guests Use the Widget

From a guest's perspective, the widget is simple to use:
1.  They select their desired check-in and check-out dates.
2.  The widget shows them the total price.
3.  They fill in their contact information.
4.  Depending on the mode, they either submit a request or complete the payment to confirm the booking.
