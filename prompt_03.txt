2. `lib/core/theme/app_typography.dart`:

3. `lib/core/theme/app_theme.dart`:

4. `lib/core/constants/app_dimensions.dart`:

   - Breakpoints (mobile: 0-600, tablet: 601-1024, desktop: 1025+)
